Overview

Purpose

We believe that roller skating is for anyone looking to try it out. We want to offer summer classes or private sessions for those who would like to improve their skills. We will have two different rinks one for beginners and another one set aside for intermediate and advanced skaters. Come on the weekends or reserve a party for an extra special experience including various themes, music and food. Check out our website daily to find the newest and hottest deals! We know some people prefer skating over blading; don’t worry we offer both! At our stop and shop center you can purchase fully customizable skates or blades. Don’t stress about it just roll with it!

Audience

We want to seem pleasing and inviting to age groups from as early as 5-25! We live in a college town with a numerous amount of young adults who are looking for fun activites to do with thier friends! Here at Roll with it Skating we also want the little children ages 5 and up to want to get into skating! Whatever your age we believe we have something fun for you!

Content

Home page

Our Story. In 2018, we begin our journey to start the first ever fully runned rollar skating company in Rexburg, Idaho. We spent many endless months trying to create the place of our dreams. This journey felt endless and was very tiring. There were several points in time when we wanted to give up. We weren't sure how we could make this a reality Finally at the beginning of 2019 we were able to bring to life Roll With it Skating. We are so excited that we are finally able to see this through.We hope you will enjoy your time with us here at Roll With It Skating!

FAQ- Will my younger children be able to skate? Yes! We have a smaller and younger aged rint for younger kids who are just starting out. We also provide skate trainers for children who need the extra help. Do you provide any protective gear? Absolutely! First and foremost we want our customers to feel safe and protected! We will include protective gear like helmets, knee and elbow pads, and gloves to anyone you would like it. Do you offer any lessons or classes? As of right now we are still working on hiring professional coaches but sometime in the future yes we will have that become available! Will there be any sort of wavier that needs to signed? Yes, waviers will need to be signed for all customers who are skating. You must be 18 years or older to sign, if you are not at least 18 a parent or guardian will need to sign for you.

Parties & Deals

We offer so many exculsive deals and promotions for all settings. Every Monday night we will offer a discount for all FHE groups with 10 or more people. This deal includes two fulls hours of skating for onyl a total of $45 dollars. When you schedule for a group party you can pick one of our party packs. The first party pack includes 1 hour of skating, one small reserved room and a discounted meal of your choosing only for $70. Second option includes skating for an hour and a half, the larger party room and a discounted meal of your choosing. We have a Saturday night special which includes various themes in songs, decorations and food. From Disney, 80s, glow-in-the-dark and a fasionable Sock Hop!

Customizable

Come into our stop and shop center or purchase online. From endless colors, brands, styles and sizes you can create a perfect pair for you! When you come in and skate for 65 hours you will get a 50% discount off of your customizable stakes. At the Stop and Shop Center we also have merch which includes, clothes, bags, wheels, waterbottles, and more! We offer a one, two, and five year warrantly on skates and blades.
