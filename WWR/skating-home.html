Our Story

In 2018, we begin our journey to start the first ever fully runned roller skating company in Rexburg, Idaho. We spent many endless months trying to create the place of our dreams. There were several points in time when we wanted to give up. We weren't sure how we could make this a reality finally at the beginning of 2019 we were able to bring to life Roll With it Skating. We are so excited that we are finally able to see this through. We hope you will enjoy your time with us here at Roll With It Skating!
